Design considerations

In the process of making this website I've had to make a lot of decisions, some of them coming from my own experience using websites, and some of them things I had never considered before. For example, I ended up putting a lot of thought into the applications and implications of using a dark theme for my website. I'm currently still in the process of figuring out how to structure my website. Section breaks, side notes, nested parentheses.
