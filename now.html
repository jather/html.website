What is a "now page"?

The idea is that's it's a page that gives an idea of what I'm up to now, a page that answers the question of "what are you up to these days?". Sort of like how it's convention for websites to have an "/about" page and a "/contact" page, but for a slightly different purpore. I think it's a pretty new trend/culture/movement in personal websites that's starting to gain popularity. Credit to Derek Sivers for starting it.
