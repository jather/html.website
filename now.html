Now

I'm in Jeju, and right now it's still uncertain whether or not I'll be returning to the UK for uni in September, because if it's going to be online classes then I could end up staying here. Right now I'm working on this website, going to the gym, and trying to prepare myself for uni. I'm hanging out on Discord a lot.

I got the idea for a "now page" from Derek Sivers. The idea is that's it's a page on a personal website that gives an idea of what I'm up to now, a page that answers the question of "what are you up to these days?". Sort of like how it's convention for websites to have an /about page and a /contact page. The "now page" fills a slightly different need and I think it's a concept that's starting to gain popularity.
